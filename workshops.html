PROFESSIONAL GEEK SERIES

Individuals are responsible for providing their own computers. All courses are live, in-person instruction conducted at Geekwise Academy, which is located at Bitwise South Stadium, 700 Van Ness Ave, Fresno, California 93721. Geekwise Academy is a division of Bitwise Industries.

Workshops are free to current Geekwise Academy students & $10 per session for non-students. Freelancing for Geeks has two sessions ($20 total) and Job Stuff has three sessions ($30 total). Workshops sessions cannot be sold separately; each session will build on the one before.
